Figure 12. Multi-basin summary of dynamic-parameter behavior for β, K0, and γ. The three rows show short-term variability, roughness, and boundary saturation ratio, respectively. Short-term variability characterizes day-to-day parameter changes, roughness characterizes high-frequency fluctuations or spikes, and boundary saturation ratio characterizes how often the normalized parameter is close to its lower or upper bound. Each point represents one basin, and boxplots summarize the basin-wise distributions across models.
